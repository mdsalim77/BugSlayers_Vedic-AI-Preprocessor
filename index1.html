॥ The Duplex Method (Dwandwa)

ऊर्ध्वतिर्यग्भ्याम् (Urdhva-Tiryagbhyam)

"Vertically and crosswise" - The ancient Vedic sutra for fast multiplication and squaring. For squaring a number, we compute the Duplex of each digit group.

Duplex Formula

• Single digit a: D(a) = a²

• Two digits ab: D(ab) = 2×a×b

• Three digits abc: D(abc) = 2×a×c + b²

Live Example: 23²

D(2)
2² = 4
D(23)
2×2×3 = 12
D(3)
3² = 9
Result: 529
